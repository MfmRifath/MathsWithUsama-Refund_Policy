Refund Policy

At MathsWithUsama, we strive to provide top-quality educational resources to help students excel in Combined Mathematics. However, if you're not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a refund policy as outlined below:

Eligibility for Refunds

  • We accept returns within 3 days from the date of purchase. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.
  • Refunds are available for paid courses, tutorials, and subscriptions within 7 days of purchase.
  • To be eligible for a refund, the course or tutorial should have been used for less than 20% of its total content.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact our support team at msmusama@gmail.com within 7 days of your purchase.

Please provide your order number and the reason for requesting the refund.

Refund Process

Once we receive your request, we will review your case and respond within 3 business days.

If your refund request is approved, the amount will be credited back to the payment-initiated method within 5-10 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • One-on-one tutoring sessions and downloadable materials (e.g., PDF notes) are non-refundable.
  • Promotional or discounted courses are non-refundable, unless otherwise stated.

Course Cancellations

In the event that MathsWithUsama cancels or discontinues a course or service, you will be eligible for a full refund, regardless of usage.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our refund policy, please contact us at msmusama@gmail.com.
